# Employee Handbook v3.0 — Welcome to Synaptiq

## Chapter 1: Company Culture and Values

At Synaptiq, we believe in building technology that augments human intelligence. Our core values are: Innovation First, Radical Transparency, Customer Obsession, and Continuous Learning. Every team member is expected to embody these values.

## Chapter 2: Work Environment

We operate a hybrid work model. Employees are expected to be in the office Tuesday through Thursday, with Monday and Friday as remote-optional days. Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM in your local timezone.

## Chapter 3: Communication Standards

- Slack is our primary async communication tool
- Confluence for documentation and knowledge sharing
- GitHub for code review and technical decisions
- Weekly all-hands meetings every Friday at 11 AM PT

## Chapter 4: Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted quarterly using the OKR (Objectives and Key Results) framework. Each employee sets 3–5 objectives per quarter with measurable key results. Reviews include self-assessment, peer feedback, and manager evaluation.

## Chapter 5: Professional Development

Each employee receives a $3,000 annual learning budget for conferences, courses, certifications, and books. Additionally, engineers get 20% time (one day per week) for personal projects, open-source contributions, or skill development.

## Chapter 6: Code of Conduct

All employees must adhere to our anti-harassment policy, data protection guidelines, and intellectual property agreements. Violations should be reported to HR or through our anonymous ethics hotline.
